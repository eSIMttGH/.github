# eSIMtt - Tap, Travel, Connect

eSIMtt provides affordable eSIM data plans for travelers, remote workers, and international users who need reliable mobile data without physical SIM cards or expensive roaming charges.

## Features

* Instant eSIM delivery
* Quick QR code activation
* Coverage across 100+ destinations
* Flexible local, regional, and global plans
* Compatible with eSIM-supported smartphones, tablets, and other devices
* No contracts or hidden fees

## How It Works

1. Choose your destination and data plan.
2. Complete your purchase.
3. Receive your eSIM instantly.
4. Scan the QR code and activate your plan.
5. Stay connected wherever you travel.
